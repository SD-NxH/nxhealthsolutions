Featured Article: Purdue Chicken Nuggets Recalled


Date: August 16, 2024

This last Friday, Purdue Foods is recalling over 165,000 pounds of frozen chicken nuggets and chicken tenders due to possible contamination with metal wire. The recall affects 22 ounce and 29 ounce packages of tenders and chicken nuggets. Check your packages for a best if used by date of March 25th, 2025. These packages were all made on the same day in March 2024.Since many of these products have been kept frozen for a really long time, the USDA urges customers to check their freezers. Stay safe and spread the word.
